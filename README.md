# Careerify

## Welcome
Careerify is a simple job matching site with two main users:
- **Students** look for jobs, save favorites, upload a resume, and apply.
- **Employers** post jobs, search candidates, review profiles, and message applicants.

## How to Use It
1. Open the site in your browser.
2. You’ll land on the login page.
3. If you don’t have an account yet, click the registration link.

## Registering
1. Fill out your username, email, and password.
2. Choose your role:
   - **student** for job seekers
   - **employer** for companies
3. Add basic profile details like location and visa status if you want.
4. Submit the form and you’re ready to go.

## Logging In
1. Enter your email and password on the login page.
2. After signing in, the site sends you to the right dashboard based on your role.

## For Students
- Browse job listings.
- Use search and filters to narrow results.
- Save jobs you like.
- Apply directly from the job list.
- Update your profile and upload your resume.
- Track your applications and rate past jobs.

## For Employers
- Post new job openings.
- Search applicants using skills, location, and visa status.
- Open applicant profiles and view resumes.
- Send messages or schedule interviews.

## What You’ll See
- A login page when you first arrive
- A registration page if you’re new
- A student dashboard with jobs and profile tools
- An employer dashboard with job posting and candidate tools

## Tips
- Students should keep their profile updated for better matches.
- Employers should use the search tools to find qualified candidates fast.
- Resumes open directly in the browser for easy review.

## Quick Note
The site is built so students and employers each get their own dashboard and features.
